FOUNDATION FOR CHILDCARE


- COVID-19 ORPHAN RELIEF -

The Child Care Foundation is providing food, medicine, education, and care to more than 1700 children affected by COVID-19. They require care and protection. The organization offers food, medication, and education to help these children whose parents have lost their lives.

Your donation today can provide life-saving assistance. For every 100 rupees you donate, one child will not go hungry.



It's not the physical pain that endangers orphans the most

It is the mental pain caused by stress from years and years of being neglected, pushed aside, disregarded, unloved, and made to feel undeserving, and in almost all cases, made to feel like a possession rather than an equal human being.
